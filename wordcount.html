Why Use a Word Counter?

Whether you are writing a blog post, academic essay, social media caption, or email newsletter, knowing your word count matters. Our free online word counter gives you real-time stats including character count, sentence count, paragraph count, estimated reading time, and a readability score to help you write better content.
