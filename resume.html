Resume Building Tips & FAQ

Professional Resume Tips

  • Keep your resume to 1-2 pages maximum
  • Use action verbs to describe achievements
  • Quantify results with numbers and percentages
  • Tailor keywords to match job descriptions
  • Choose a clean, professional template
  • Proofread carefully for spelling and grammar

For Freelancers & Contractors

Highlight project-based work and diverse skill sets. Include contract durations and client results.
