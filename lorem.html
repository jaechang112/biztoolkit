What Is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is placeholder text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. Derived from Cicero's "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum," it provides a natural-looking distribution of letters and words, making it ideal for previewing layouts without distracting readers with meaningful content.
